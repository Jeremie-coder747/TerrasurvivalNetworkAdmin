About the Server

Terra Survival Network is a friendly, community-based Minecraft SMP focused on fair survival gameplay, creativity, and long-term worlds.

This server is not dead! We're actively maintained and updated with a growing community of dedicated players.

We support both Java and Bedrock editions so everyone can join. Come hang out, build something amazing, and make new friends.

Play Nice

Server Rules

01
No Griefing
No destroying others' builds, letting creepers blow things up, using TNT, or causing lag with redstone machines.
02
No Stealing
Don't take from others' chests or claim their homes and structures.
03
No Killing Others' Pets
Instant ban. Period.
04
No Spamming or Advertising
No Discord invites, websites, or malicious links in chat. Contact staff for partnership.
05
No Unfinished Builds
Keep the world clean. Finish what you start or remove it.
06
No NSFW Builds or Signs
Keep it appropriate. No vulgar signs or inappropriate structures.
07
Respect Others' Space
No entering homes without permission. No spawn killing unless both players agree to PvP.
08
No Hacking or Exploiting
No flying hacks, X-ray, Baritone bots, or any other cheats. Play fair.
09
No Harassment or Slurs
Mild swearing is fine. Zero tolerance for slurs, discrimination, or targeted harassment.
10
No Loophole Abuse
If something's clearly wrong, don't do it — even if not listed. Use common sense.
01
Follow Discord's TOS
All Discord Terms of Service apply, plus our additional rules.
02
No Spamming
Don't spam messages. You'll get muted.
03
No Harassment
No slurs, discrimination, or targeted harassment of any kind.
04
No NSFW Content
No porn, gore, or inappropriate content. Server must be suitable for users 13+.
05
No Personal Information
Don't share or ask for names, phone numbers, or financial details.
06
No Advertising
No posting Discord invites or external links without staff approval.
07
No Recording Voice Channels
Do not record voice channels without consent of everyone present.
08
No Loophole Abuse
If it's not right, don't do it. USE YOUR COMMON SENSE.
